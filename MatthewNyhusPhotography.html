About

Welcome to my photography site! The purpose of the site isn't primarily to showcase my pictures, but more to prototype a new style of photography websites that showcase not just the finished product, but also the work that went into editing the picture. I assume that every picture I see online is edited, so instead of trying to hide the process, I thought it would be interesting to show that part off.

To start, simply select a picture you like. Then, you can scroll through the different stages of the picture or, by selecting the toggle, see a before and after reveal by dragging on the picture.

Thanks for visiting!
